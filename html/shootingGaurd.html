Top Guards in the league

Here Are the top 3 Shooting Gaurds currently in the NBA according to stats from ESPN.com

Luka Doncic

Luka Doncic has been on an incredible 2022-2023 season, being the first ever player to get a 60/20/10 triple double. With his high Assist Ratio, shot percentage, and incredible playmaking skills; Luka deserves to be on the top of this list.

Number 1

Shai Gilgeous-Alexander

Shai Gilgeous-Alexander has been having a tremendous season this year. Averaging 30.9 points per game and a high Field goal percentage, Shai makes his way to the number 2 spot.

Number 2

Damian Lillard

Damian Lillard has also had a great 2023 season. With a Higher point average then Shai, but lower field goal percentage. With slightly worse stats to Shai, Damian should be right behind him at number 3.

Number 3

Stephen Curry

Steph Curry is always a threat on the court, with the highest true shooting percentage of any point guard in the league. Still, his efficiency this season leaves more to be desired, putting him at number 4 on this list.

Number 4

Ja Morant

Every season, Ja Morant puts in more and more work for Memphis with his highest point, assist, and rebound rate ever. His efficiency is leagues better than most other point guards, getting him the number 5 spot.
